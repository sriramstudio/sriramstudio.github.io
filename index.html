About the Studio

Sriram Studio is a performing arts academy in Kolkata founded in 2012 by Anjali Saharia — a classically trained Odissi artist with over 20 years of training under Guru Poushali Mukherjee. With 400+ active students across three locations, the studio offers rigorous classical training alongside a broad range of dance styles, built on the belief that dance education should nurture discipline, creativity, and confidence — and that it is never too late, nor too early, to begin.

Sriram Studio is affiliated to Pracheen Kala Kendra, Chandigarh — students appear for board examinations and receive certified credentials in classical dance.
